For a seamless login experience, you can set up and link a personal LastPass account. Personal = Private. Your Enterprise Admin never sees what you store in your personal account, and your personal account goes with you when you leave the company.

Once you link the accounts, your personal account will appear as a sub-folder in your Enterprise vault. You can save all personal logins directly into your personal folder, or drag them in later!

If you don't have a personal account, create one now.
